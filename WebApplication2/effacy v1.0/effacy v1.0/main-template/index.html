What I can do

Just to give you an idea of a few of my services, here's a list of things I could do for you

Web Development / Web Design

Your website is the digital face of your entire business. If it’s not built to maximize lead generation, insights, analytics, sales and online presence–while still looking good across all browsers, devices and screen sizes, it’s not the business asset it should be. My Web Development and Design services can improve every aspect of your digital business.

Content Marketing

Content is found on social media, websites, blogs, emails, and more. Developing helpful, useful, and interesting content helps strengthen your business’ story and get that story heard by the right people. It gives your potential customers a reason to engage with your business beyond a quick visit to your website, and your current customers a reason to stick around. Position your business above your competitors by educating and entertaining your audience into become advocates of your brand with content marketing. Your online content impacts the way your prospects and customers find, think, and feel about your brand. Creative, innovative content combined with Search Engine Optimization and Social Media best practices is a killer combination that makes your business easy to find and attracts, then engages your target audience. Our Content Marketing service puts your brand in front of prospects, then convinces them to take your desired action by catering to their needs and solving their pain points through highly targeted, creative, top quality digital content.

Digital Media Production / Online Video

Online Video represents an opportunity for savvy Digital Web Services companies to cash in on an emerging industry. Because the internet dominates all forms of media during the purchase process, online video capitalises on a uniquely engaged, active audience. Testimonials, announcements, webinars, product demonstrations, commercials and RSS Feeds account for only a small proportion of online video use. Photography, graphic design, print media, busines cards etc.

Social Media Marketing / Management

Twitter. Facebook. Google+. Instagram. Management of all your Social Media profiles and properties can be confusing and time consuming, but I can solve that for you. I have a complete understanding of what it takes to leverage social media platforms to communicate with your customers and amplify your online brand presence.

Email Marketing

Newsletters, mail-outs, invitations, promotions and business communications all fall under the umbrella term ’email marketing’ and provide cost-effective, measureable Digital Web Services alternatives. If you’re looking to add email marketing to part of your integrated online marketing program, our team of results-driven professionals can work with you to deliver a customer-centric program geared at converting and retaining customers. We’ve honed our email marketing process to create a results-driven approach to messaging, Call To Action (CTA), lead capture and conversion metrics.

Analytics (Web, Mobile, Social)

Quite simply, if you’re not measuring the progress of your web, mobile and social media marketing campaigns, you’re missing out on valuable information and insights about your customer’s likes, dislikes and spending habits. Once you know these things, you can tailor your offerings to maximize lead generation, customer retention and sales. I know my Analytics and I can help you capitalize on them.

Mobile App Devlopment

According to the Supreme Court, “There’s an app for that” is officially part of the public lexicon. In the age of the smartphone, your business needs to be optimized for access on mobile devices. The easiest way for you to do that is to let me do that for you

e-Commerce Solutions

The benefits of selling online cannot be overstated. You open yourself up to the largest possible customer base while not incurring the rising costs of multiple physical locations. eCommerce is becoming the norm for many consumers looking for specialized goods and services across the world. With mobile activity continuing to take precedence over desktop, consumers are craving the convenience of ecommerce experiences more than ever; and it won’t stop here. Even search engines like Google and social networks like Pinterest and Instagram are making strides toward offering the invaluable “Buy” button to users. Mainstreethost helps plan for the growth of your business by developing ecommerce storefronts that bring an enjoyable shopping experience right to your customers’ hands.

Branding

A brand identity is much more than just a logo and color palette, it’s what sets an organization, person, product or service apart. Our branding work is always process-driven – and not just any process. It’s the same process used to craft the biggest and best brands in the world. While some companies will claim to have a “proprietary” brand building process, ours is tried-and-true. I'll walk you through the process. I'll research, strategize, design, implement, then guide you on further implementation.

Search Engine Marketing

Search Engine Optimization is essential for long term success, but if you’re not properly capitalizing on pay-per-click, paid search marketing across channels like Google, Bing and Yahoo!, you’re missing out on valuable data and insights. I'll provide everything you need for a successful Search Engine Marketing campaign.

SEO Optimization

Search engines are the greatest resource for potential customers looking for information. With properly configured keywords, ad copy, landing page design, server side optimizations and potential web development / design, with my help, I can improve your placement across all search engine platforms.
